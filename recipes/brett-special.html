Brett Special

Description

The famously healthy and delicious dish prepared by Brett Tanner for his every meal in Japan.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Frozen Veggies

Steps

  1. Cook rice in a rice cooker
  2. As rice is finishing, microwave frozen veggies for 5min
  3. Mix rice, veggies, cheese and the pre-cooked meat
  4. Hot rice heats the meat and pleasantly melts cheese
  5. Enjoy!
